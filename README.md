# machine_learning_customer_churn
Machine Learning about Customer Churn. Customer churn refers to the phenomenon where customers discontinue their relationship or subscription with a company or service provider. In the context of the Churn dataset, the churn label indicates whether a customer has churned or not. 
Three models available to practice and compare the difference between performance of different models in predicting customer churn result.
Decision Tree is performed by-scratch, while others are performed with predefined models.
